## Open Source Projects

Heurist’s open-source frontends integrate with our LLM and Image Generation APIs, providing powerful templates and inspiration for your own projects. These apps showcase the potential of Heurist Protocol and serve as learning resources for the community.

-  [**Heurist Imagine**](https://github.com/heurist-network/imagine) – AI-powered image generator supporting fine-tuned Stable Diffusion models and Flux.
-  [**Pondera**](https://github.com/heurist-network/pondera) – AI chatbot offering experiences similar to ChatGPT or HuggingFace Chat.
-  **Heurist Search** – AI-enhanced search engine that leverages LLMs to summarize and synthesize internet search results.

  - [Frontend](https://github.com/heurist-network/gpt-search-web)
  - [Backend](https://github.com/heurist-network/search_with_lepton)

-  [**Babel Bot**](https://github.com/heurist-network/babel-discord-bot) – A Discord bot powered by LLMs, facilitating seamless language translation.
