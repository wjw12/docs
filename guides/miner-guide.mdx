## Official miner software: [https://github.com/heurist-network/miner-release](https://github.com/heurist-network/miner-release)

Make sure you have NVIDIA GPU and CUDA driver installed. At least 100Mbps upload speed is recommended.

## LLM Miner

#### Top Picks for NVIDIA RTX 3090 / 4090 with 24GB VRAM

* **Dolphin-2.9-llama3-8b /** **hermes-3-llama3.1-8b / theia-llama-3.1-8b**

  * Full-precision Llama3 8b model

  * Perfect for miners with consumer GPUs looking for consistent returns

#### Top Picks for GPUs with >= 40GB VRAM (A6000, 6000Ada, A100, H100)

* **openhermes-mistral-8x7b-gptq**

  * 4-bit quantized version of Mistral 8x7b model

  * Ideal for those with high-end hardware seeking maximum mining efficiency

## Stable Diffusion Miner

* **Minimum Requirement**: NVIDIA cards with at least 12GB of VRAM

* Host models to help artists and creators generate stunning visuals
