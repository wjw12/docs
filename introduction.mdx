## Introducing Heurist

Heurist is a decentralized AI-as-a-Service cloud. We aggregate compute resources from individual GPU owners and data centers to provide serverless AI services. Heuristâ€™s API-first infrastructure elinimates the need for developers to manage GPU machines, and enables cost-efficient, censorship-free AI integration with APIs.

### Key Offerings

- **Elasticity in Compute Resources and in Mining Emission**: Heurist dynamically scales compute resources to meet fluctuating demand, and adopts an adaptive token emission mechanism that responds to network activity. Elasticity in these two aspects ensures optimal resource utilization and economic stability.

- **Serverless AI-as-a-Service**: Heurist abstracts away the complexities of hardware management. Deploy workloads and access AI services through intuitive APIs and SDKs with a few lines of code, saving the hassle of managing GPU machines.

- **Alignment-Centric Economics (ACE)**: Our economic model aligns all participants towards shared goals: expanding compute resources, optimizing utilization, advancing AI models, and preserving long-term protocol value. Heurist Token is tightly integrated into every interaction in the ecosystem.

- **Web3-Native AI Ecosystem**: By supporting and incubating AI applications targeted to crypto users, such as finetuned models for Web3 IPs, onchain/offchain agents with crypto knowledge, AIGC NFTs Heurist brings cutting-edge AI to the crypto world.
